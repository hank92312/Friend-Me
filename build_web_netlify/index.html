Friends & Me

異步社交探索桌遊

透過自我揭露與社交驗證，促進朋友間的深度連結與自我探索。

🧠 遊戲設計理念

本遊戲基於喬哈里視窗（Johari Window）心理學理論設計。透過「自我揭露」與「社交驗證」雙向互動，幫助玩家探索他人眼中的自己與真實自我之間的默契落差，開啟深度、溫馨且有意義的對話。

🔒 心理安全與隱私保障

我們深知揭露自我的社交焦慮，因此設計了「不回答」機制，讓玩家在不願分享特定話題時能體面拒絕，同時作為配對時的干擾項。所有對局數據僅在伺服器隨機存取記憶體 (RAM) 中暫存同步，一旦玩家離線或遊戲結束即徹底擦除，零永久儲存，保障您的絕對隱私。

☕ 社交話題深度分級 (Level 1 - 5)

遊戲提供 5 個不同的社交級別話題，適配各種聚會情境：

  • Level 1：閒話家常 - 關於日常習慣與輕鬆偏好。
  • Level 2：下午茶閒聊 - 適合普通朋友與聚會破冰。
  • Level 3：居酒屋微醺 - 稍微深入的感情與生活交流。
  • Level 4：深夜真心話 - 屬於摯友或伴侶間的夜深小秘密。
  • Level 5：靈魂拷問 - 挑戰道德邊界或極端情境的深度思辨。

🎮 四大核心遊玩步驟

  1. 創建/加入房間：快速輸入暱稱，一鍵創建 6 位數房間碼並發送給好友。
  2. 選題與答題：隊長隨機選題，所有玩家在限時內填寫答案或選擇不回答。
  3. 社交配對猜測：拖曳玩家大頭貼與對應的匿名答案進行配對。
  4. 精彩結算揭曉：觀看精緻的猜中率百分比動畫，統計每局的默契得分。

❓ 常見問答 (FAQ)

問：這款遊戲適合多少人玩？
答：本遊戲適合 2 至 6 人同時連線遊玩，是家庭聚會、派對或情侶互動的絕佳選擇。

問：網頁版與 App 版有什麼區別？
答：網頁版即開即玩，方便快捷；App 版效能更穩定，且支援 Google Play 應用內購買「移除廣告」方案，提供更為純淨的無廣告遊戲體驗。

🧠 Core Vision & Concept

Designed around the psychological concept of the Johari Window. Through gameplay, players disclose aspects of themselves and verify how their friends perceive them, resolving the gap between self-perception and public-perception in a fun, meaningful way.

🔒 Psychological Safety & Privacy

To reduce social anxiety, players can select "Prefer Not to Answer" to skip sensitive questions. This option acts as a distractor during the matchmaking phase. All session data is cached temporarily in the server's RAM and is permanently erased once the session ends. No database records are kept.

☕ Topics Depth Levels (Level 1 - 5)

Features 5 progressive difficulty levels to match any social scenario:

  • Level 1: Casual Chat - Daily habits and light preferences.
  • Level 2: Afternoon Tea - Icebreakers and casual catch-ups.
  • Level 3: Izakaya Drunk - Relationship stories and life experiences.
  • Level 4: Late-Night Secrets - Deep personal shares for close friends.
  • Level 5: Soul Search - Extreme scenarios and ethical dilemmas.

🎮 Four Core Gameplay Steps

  1. Lobby Setup: Enter a nickname and generate a 6-digit room code to invite friends.
  2. Answer Questions: The leader picks a level, and players type their answers or choose to skip.
  3. Matchmaking Guess: Link player avatars with their guessed answers in a matching interface.
  4. Score Revelation: Watch real-time percentage animation counting correct guesses.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How many players are supported?
A: Best played with 2 to 6 players simultaneously, perfect for party meetups and couples.

Q: What is the difference between Web and App?
A: Web version loads instantly in browser. The App version provides offline caching and includes an optional "Remove Ads" in-app purchase (1 USD / 30 TWD) to remove all interstitial ads.
