# Friends & Me Core Question Bank (Seed Question Bank - V4 International Edition)

## Project Info

- **Project Name**: Friends & Me
- **Purpose**: Initial question bank dataset for AI Agents or backend systems.
- **Design Logic**: Social Penetration Theory (All open-ended questions designed to ensure answer distinctiveness and avoid predictable responses).

---

## Level 1: Everyday Chit-Chat (Daily habits & lifestyle)

*Psychological Goal: Establish basic psychological safety and observe each other's daily rhythms and micro-habits.*

1. **Breakfast Preference**: If you had to eat the exact same thing for breakfast every day without getting sick of it, what specific food would you choose?
2. **Wind-down Ritual**: Describe your typical "wind-down ritual" in the last 30 minutes before you go to sleep.
3. **Shower Routine**: When you take a shower, what is the very first body part you wash?
4. **Phone Dependency**: When you wake up, what is the very first app you open on your phone?
5. **Food Taboo**: Name one ingredient that absolutely does not belong on a pizza (or in your favorite dish), and briefly explain why.
6. **Commute**: What is your most common habit or activity when riding public transportation or commuting?
7. **Wardrobe Style**: If you open your closet right now, what specific color or style of clothing takes up the most space?
8. **Dream Home**: With an unlimited budget, what does your absolute dream living environment look like (e.g., a beachfront cabin, a high-rise penthouse)?
9. **Shopping Habit**: What was the last item you bought as a pure "impulse purchase"?
10. **Exercise**: If you were forced to work out, what specific physical activity would you dread the least?
11. **Coffee Order**: When you go to a coffee shop or cafe, what is your exact, highly specific go-to order?
12. **Cleaning Habit**: How often do you do a deep, thorough clean of your bedroom or apartment?
13. **Travel Role**: In a group trip, what specific role do you unconsciously take on (e.g., the human GPS, the foodie, the one who sleeps in)?
14. **Digital Habits**: How many unread notifications do you currently have on your messaging app?
15. **Weather Preference**: What specific type of weather or temperature instantly puts you in a good mood?
16. **Travel Essential**: When traveling far from home, what specific "non-essential, but comforting" item must you pack in your suitcase?
17. **Signature Dish**: If you had to cook your signature dish to host friends today, what specific meal would you make?
18. **Gaming Quirk**: When playing video games or board games, what specific quirky habit do you have (e.g., exploring every corner of the map, hoarding items, or rushing the main quest)?
19. **Pet Fantasy**: If you could adopt any pet right now (ignoring space, money, or climate constraints), what specific animal would you choose?
20. **Convenience Store**: If you walk into a convenience store or gas station just to grab a quick craving, what is your go-to snack or drink?
21. **Morning Trigger**: Within the first hour of waking up, what is the one specific thing you must do, otherwise your day hasn't "truly started"?
22. **Desk Ecosystem**: What specific, non-practical item must you have on your desk or workspace just to make yourself happy?
23. **Fashion Fixation**: When shopping for clothes, shoes, or bags, what specific "design detail" or "material" inexplicably draws you in?
24. **Appliance Choice**: If you could only keep one non-essential "quality-of-life" small appliance in your home, which one would it be?
25. **Street Observation**: When walking alone down the street, what specific thing usually catches your eye (e.g., typography on signs, people's outfits, stray animals)?
26. **Night Market Must-Eat**: When you stroll through a night market, what is the one snack you almost always buy?
27. **Breakfast Shop Order**: At your go-to local breakfast shop, what is your usual fixed order?
28. **Rainy Day Gear**: When it's pouring rain and you have to go out, what is your standard gear or survival strategy?
29. **Lunchbox Philosophy**: At a buffet or bento shop, what is the one side dish you always grab?
30. **Typhoon Day Off**: When a surprise day off is announced (like a typhoon day), how do you usually spend it?
31. **Bubble Tea Loyalty**: What is your one true go-to hand-shaken drink order?
32. **Summer Survival**: When summer gets unbearably hot and humid, what is your most relied-upon way to cool down?
33. **Reunion Dinner Dish**: At a big family reunion dinner (like Lunar New Year's Eve), which dish do you look forward to the most?
34. **Midnight Snack**: When you suddenly get hungry late at night, what is your go-to midnight snack?
35. **Mid-Autumn Tradition**: During the Mid-Autumn Festival, what is the one activity that always happens with your family or friends?

## Level 2: Happy Hour / Pub Talk (Personal flair & social preferences)

*Psychological Goal: Showcase unique tastes, project interests, and share views on the outside world.*

1. **Dream Vacation**: With an unlimited budget and a full week off, which country would you most want to visit?
2. **First Impression**: What do you think is the most common first impression people have of you?
3. **Social Battery**: When your social battery runs out at a large gathering, what specific excuse or method do you use to slip away?
4. **Movie Taste**: What is a movie you love so much that you've watched it more than three times?
5. **Work Motivation**: Aside from the paycheck, what specific aspect of your job gives you the most sense of accomplishment?
6. **Idol/Role Model**: Which public figure or fictional character do you admire the most?
7. **Gifting Philosophy**: What is the most successful, surprising gift you've ever given someone that you are still proud of?
8. **Comfort Food**: When you're feeling mildly stressed, what specific food instantly comforts you?
9. **Instant Skill**: If you could instantly master a new language or musical instrument, which one would you choose?
10. **Social Red Flag**: In a social setting, what specific behavior instantly makes you lose respect for someone?
11. **Ideal Weekend**: What does your perfect Saturday afternoon look like?
12. **Animal Alter Ego**: If you had to describe yourself as an animal, what would you be?
13. **Fire Rescue**: If your house were on fire and you could only save one non-electronic personal item, what would it be?
14. **Restaurant Dealbreaker**: When picking a restaurant for a group dinner, what specific type of place or "red flag" would make you absolutely avoid it?
15. **Social Media**: Which social media platform do you use the most frequently?
16. **AI Assistant**: If an AI robot could take over exactly one daily chore you despise, what specific task would you assign it?
17. **Side Hustle**: If you were to start a completely unprofitable, niche "side hustle" or hobby, what would it be?
18. **Virtual Isekai**: If you could live inside the universe of any video game, movie, or anime for a month, which specific world would you choose?
19. **Saving the Vibe**: In a group hangout, if the conversation suddenly goes dead silent, what specific topic or action do you use to save the vibe?
20. **Found Money**: If you found $50 on the street and had to blow it purely on entertainment today, what exactly would you do with it?
21. **Content Creator**: If you were forced to start a YouTube channel or Podcast today, what specific topic would you cover?
22. **VIP Tickets**: If you could get free, front-row VIP tickets to any live event (concert, theater, sports), who or what would you go see?
23. **Micro-Achievement**: In the past year, what is the smallest, most trivial achievement that you felt secretly proud of for a long time?
24. **Internet Rabbit Hole**: When browsing the internet, what specific type of clickbait title or video genre always gets you to click and go down a rabbit hole?
25. **Nostalgia Track**: Name a specific song or soundtrack that instantly teleports you back to a specific memory from your school days.
26. **Taste of Taiwan**: If you could take a foreign friend to eat one food that best represents Taiwan, what would it be?
27. **Hidden Gem**: Which spot in Taiwan (main island or offshore) is your hidden gem that you'd love to revisit?
28. **City Swap**: If you had to move away from where you live now, which city or county in Taiwan would you choose?
29. **Local Screen Gem**: Which Taiwanese drama or film would you most recommend to a friend?
30. **Karaoke Safe Pick**: At karaoke (KTV), what is your "safe bet" go-to song?
31. **Childhood Snack**: Name one childhood snack or old-school treat you still can't resist buying today?
32. **Nostalgic TV**: Which TV show did you never miss back when you were a student?
33. **Worth the Line**: What specific food would you willingly queue more than 30 minutes for?
34. **Night Market Champion**: Among night market game stalls (balloon darts, goldfish scooping, pinball...), which one are you most confident at?
35. **New Year's Eve Plan**: What is your ideal way to spend the New Year's Eve countdown?

## Level 3: Deep Conversations (Emotional resonance & life complaints)

*Psychological Goal: Lower defenses, share emotional experiences, and reveal inner turbulence.*

1. **Tearjerker**: What was the last movie or book that actually made you cry?
2. **Workplace Grievance**: What specific behavior from a boss or coworker is absolutely intolerable to you?
3. **Romance Priority**: When looking for a partner, what specific trait or core value do you prioritize above all else?
4. **Time Machine**: If you could turn back time, which specific stage of your life would you return to and make a different choice?
5. **Safety Net**: When you are feeling anxious, who is the very first person you want to call for comfort?
6. **Friendship Boundary**: What specific action by a friend would make you cut ties with them completely?
7. **Childhood Insecurity**: Growing up, what specific trait of yours were you the most insecure about?
8. **Love Language**: What is a specific small detail—something your partner did or said—that made you feel profoundly loved?
9. **Career Low**: What was the single most regretful job you’ve ever taken?
10. **Anger Expression**: When you are extremely angry, what specific behavior or reaction do you typically display?
11. **Peer Pressure**: Describe a time you pretended to like something just to fit in or please a group.
12. **Family Impact**: Which specific aspect of your parents' personalities has had the deepest impact on you?
13. **First Love**: When you think of your first love, what is the very first keyword that pops into your head?
14. **Life Motivation**: When you are completely exhausted, what is the core motivation that keeps you pushing forward?
15. **Solo Adventure**: What is the most "out-of-your-comfort-zone" activity you have ever done completely by yourself (e.g., going to a concert, eating at a fancy sit-down restaurant)?
16. **Breaking Point**: What recent, specific life event gave you a deep sense of powerlessness or almost made you "snap"?
17. **Unsaid Apology**: Looking back at your past relationships, what specific kind of person (or specific individual) do you feel you owe a sincere apology to?
18. **Relationship Friction**: In an intimate relationship (partner or best friend), what kind of "trivial everyday thing" most easily makes you secretly sulk?
19. **Peer Anxiety**: Seeing people around you achieve what "specific milestone or status" most easily triggers your own anxiety?
20. **Rock Bottom Support**: After experiencing a terrible setback, what "specific action" do you want friends to take to support you (instead of just saying "cheer up")?
21. **Stereotype**: What is the most common misconception people have about your personality, which is entirely untrue on the inside?
22. **Fork in the Road**: Looking back, what seemingly insignificant "tiny decision" ended up drastically altering the trajectory of your life?
23. **Compromise**: What is the biggest "break of your own rules" or compromise you’ve ever made for a friend or partner?
24. **Closure Ritual**: When dealing with "loss" (a relationship, an environment, or an object), what specific ritual do you need to go through to truly move on?
25. **Secret Pride**: Name a slightly "dark" or "arrogant" trait in your personality that you actually kind of like about yourself.
26. **Relatives' Greatest Hits**: At family gatherings, which classic line of "concern" from elders makes you roll your eyes inside?
27. **Normalized Absurdity**: What is one thing in everyday life that "everyone treats as normal" but quietly fills you with helplessness?
28. **Comparison Shadow**: Growing up, who were you most often compared to by your parents or elders?
29. **Workplace Unwritten Rule**: What is the one unwritten rule of workplace culture you most want to change?
30. **Taste of Home**: When living away from home (for school, work, or long travel), what specific thing from home do you miss the most?
31. **Left on Read**: When someone important leaves you on read, what little drama usually plays out in your head?
32. **Student-Era Regret**: What is the one thing you most regret NOT doing back in your school days?
33. **Friendship Expiry**: Think of a friendship that slowly faded — what do you think was the real reason it faded?
34. **In the Name of Love**: Which "it's for your own good" line puts the most pressure on you?
35. **Holiday Burnout**: During the big holiday season (like Lunar New Year), which part exhausts you the most?

## Level 4: Late Night Confessions (Secrets, hidden worries & true vulnerability)

*Psychological Goal: Deep disclosure, touching upon the vulnerable self and hidden truths.*

1. **Deepest Fear**: What is the deepest fear hidden in your heart that you are always avoiding facing?
2. **Midnight Secret**: Share a tiny secret you’ve buried in your heart for a long time and never confessed to your friends.
3. **Hidden Quirk**: Write down a specific quirk or habit you have that nobody in this room knows about.
4. **Final Words**: If you were to pass away tomorrow, who is the one person you most want to say "I'm sorry" to right now?
5. **Self-Criticism**: What is the one flaw in your own personality that you hate the most?
6. **Social Mask**: What kind of facade do you typically use to protect yourself when around strangers?
7. **Rebellious Streak**: What is the craziest, most unconventional thing you have ever done in your life?
8. **Building Trust**: In interpersonal relationships, what specific event or behavior instantly makes you build deep trust with someone?
9. **Unfulfilled Dream**: What is a dream you were forced to abandon for practical reasons, but still long for?
10. **Alienation**: When was the last time you felt completely out of place and disconnected from the world?
11. **Selfish Moment**: Confess the most selfish thing you have ever done but never told anyone about.
12. **Psychological Shadow**: What specific childhood experience still impacts your adult life today?
13. **Social Burnout**: When you are completely burnt out from socializing and just want to disappear, where do you go or what do you do to heal yourself?
14. **Defining Success**: Setting fame and wealth aside, what does true "success" look like in your heart?
15. **Flashes of Jealousy**: Honestly speaking, what specific achievement or possession of a close friend has ever caused you to feel a brief flash of jealousy?
16. **Good News Only**: What real situation do you most often hide from your family?
17. **Midnight Search**: What category of search keywords have you secretly looked up late at night that you'd never want anyone to see?
18. **Thickest Mask**: In which recurring setting (office, family gatherings, reunions...) do you wear your thickest mask?
19. **Money Secret**: What money situation or spending habit do you have that even your closest people don't know about?
20. **Secret Tears**: The last time you cried in secret behind everyone's back, what was it about?
21. **Social Alibi**: What excuse do you most often use to cover up the fact that you simply don't want to socialize?
22. **Words of Guilt**: Share one sentence you once blurted out that still makes you feel guilty today.
23. **Impostor Trophy**: Which achievement or advantage that others envy do you secretly feel you don't deserve?
24. **Unspoken Words**: What is the one sentence to your family that you've kept buried and never managed to say?
25. **Disguised Compromise**: Which part of your current life are you "pretending to like" when it's actually a compromise?
26. **Solitude vs Loneliness**: In your view, what is the difference between solitude and loneliness?

## Level 5: Soul Interrogation (Core values & extreme challenges)

*Psychological Goal: Challenge boundaries, explore the meaning of life, and test moral limits.*

1. **Value Sorting**: If you had to trade the most important thing in your life (tangible or intangible) for absolute freedom, what would you trade away?
2. **Trolley Problem**: If the world were ending and you could only bring one person onto Noah's Ark, what cruel criteria would you use to choose that person?
3. **Memory Erasure**: If someone offered you ten million dollars on the condition that they erase one specific memory from your life, which memory would you choose to erase?
4. **Meaning of Life**: If you knew for a fact you only had one year left to live, exactly how would you plan your next 365 days?
5. **Immortality Choice**: If you gained the power of immortality, what "ultimate life mission" would you set for yourself?
6. **Boundary of Justice**: Name a specific action that you believe is "morally or legally wrong, but under certain circumstances, you would absolutely do it."
7. **Essence of Self**: If you had to reintroduce "who you are" to a completely amnesiac version of yourself, what would be the three most important sentences you write down?
8. **Value of Pain**: Name a specific setback that caused you immense pain, but looking back now, you are glad it happened.
9. **Hero's Sacrifice**: If your sacrifice could buy one specific change for the world, what exact change would you want your life to be traded for?
10. **Scale of Betrayal**: Under what extreme circumstances would you choose to betray your most cherished principles?
11. **Future Generations**: If the world is doomed to be destroyed in fifty years, what final message or advice would you leave for today's younger generation?
12. **Defining Loneliness**: Describe the specific moment in your life where you felt the most "ultimate, absolute loneliness."
13. **Limits of Forgiveness**: Name a specific action that, even if done by your closest friend or family member, you could absolutely never forgive.
14. **Trading Lifespan**: If you could trade ten years of your life for one supernatural ability, what exact power would you choose?
15. **The Final Question**: If you had to engrave a short phrase summarizing your entire life on your tombstone, what would you engrave?
16. **Luggage for the Next Life**: If you could carry only one memory into your next life, which one would you choose?
17. **Weight of Truth**: If you could learn one absolute truth about yourself (how people really see you, the date of your death...), what would you choose to know?
18. **Restart Keepsake**: If your life had to completely restart but you could keep one existing thing (a relationship, an ability, a memory...), what would you keep?
19. **The Unsellable Line**: Which one of your moral lines do you believe can never be bought, no matter the price offered?
20. **Vanish for a Year**: If you could anonymously disappear for one year with zero consequences, what kind of life would you live?
21. **Defending Yourself**: If you had to defend the worst thing you have ever done, what would you say?
22. **Love's Forbidden Zone**: For the person you love most, what is the one thing you absolutely could NOT do?
23. **One Question for God**: If you could ask a deity one question with a guaranteed true answer, what would you ask?
24. **The Last Keepsake**: If you could leave behind only one object for the most important person after you're gone, what would it be?
25. **Erase One Suffering**: If you could permanently erase one form of human suffering from the world (hunger, loneliness, illness, betrayal...), which would you erase?
